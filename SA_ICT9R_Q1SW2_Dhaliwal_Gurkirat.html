Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Maria Clara Maria Clara's full name is María Clara de los Santos. Everyone looked up to Maria Clara when she was a little child because she enjoys playing. Although she may seem like a happy person, she later killed herself due to her husband's death.
Kapitan Tiago Captian Tiago's Full name is Don Santíago de los Santos. He is a son of a wealthy trades and had a lot of influence.
Don Rafael Ibarra Don Rafael's Full name is Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin. In the narrative, he is described as generous and extremely wealthy.
Padre Salvi Padre Salvi's full name is Padre Bernardo Salv. In the book, he is described as being very skinny with pale skin.
Tenyente Guevarra Tinyente Guevarra's full name is Miguel Guevarra. He is a personal friend of Don Rafael Ibarra and a lieutenant in the Guardia Civil.
